TRX Training

What is TRX?
TRX -Total Body Resistance Exercises, refers to a specialized form of suspension training system that utilizes equipment made of of two straps, developed by a former U.S. Navy Seal to stay in top shape anywhere.
The straps can be hung anywhere, indoors or out, at the local park, home, office, hotel room etc.

TRX workout includes combining aerobic and anaerobic training in many enjoyable different exercises.
During workout, you can control the level of challenge by simply adjusting your body position (step backward or forward) to increase or decrease resistance.

TRX trainings are fun, developing your strength, balance, flexibility and core stability simultaneously, and are also great for conditioning.
There are endless different exercises that you can choose for your TRX training so it's never boring and always challenging.
TRX training saves time and enables maximal work in minimum time- that's due to the nature of TRX exercise - using all in 1 system.

It is highly recommended to perform the first trainings with a personal trainer that will guide you on the right use of the TRX for a proper and safe exercise.

In my sessions you will be able to choose a full TRX session or combining it as a part of an integrated workout.

Personal Running Training

Running is a basic and available activity to everyone, all you need is a pair of sneakers and a decision.

The running world is a wonderful world, you have probably noticed recently that more and more people are running. The reason is, that on top of the health benefits related to running- right from the first day of running – physiological changes occur in the body and create a marvelous sensation- physical and mental.

Why isn’t everyone running? The reason is that the first stage of deciding and starting to run is the most difficult, and without professional guidance might discourage and lead to injury.
With the support of a personal trainer you will be able to enjoy the first most difficult stage of getting into the running world.

Together with me, step by step, you will be able to run a few consecutive km, according to your goals.

Why running is good for you? Besides that it’s a great fun?
Running has so many benefits:

  • Did you know that running is "Anti Aging"? Scientific research shows that intense physical activity such as running, even among amateur exercisers, prolongs life expectancy by as much as an average of 5 years. The reason is that physical activity significantly prevents many different diseases and health conditions: cardiovascular, metabolic diseases like diabetes, and some kinds of cancer.
  • Running as a weight-bearing exercise preserves bone and muscle mass in the body and is excellent for prevention and treatment for osteoporosis (low bone mass). Running also increases growth hormone levels and described by some as the key to slowing the aging process.
  • Running improves your health, contributes to increase HDL levels and decreases blood sugar level, running improves breathing and strengthens your immune system.
  • Running is great for weight lose and maintaining your desirable body weight.
  • Running contributes tremendously to your self-image, strengthens your self confidence and improves satisfaction perception.
  • Running reduces anxiety, relieves stress, decreases depression levels and fatigue, and high intensity running eliminates aggression. Running clears your mind, and during running you will often get to solve problems that during daytime bothered you and appeared non-solvable.
  • During running, the body releases chemicals called Endorphines, that trigger a positive feeling in the body similar to drugs - which leads to a euphoric sensation, known as "Runners High".
  • Running regularly trains the consciousness and enables you to strengthen your determination and to overcome obstacles, within your exercise program as well as in your personal life.
